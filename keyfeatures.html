Reflections

Reflections on SWIFT Inu work by implementing a 10% tax on all buy and sell transactions of SWIFT Inu tokens. This tax is then divided into two parts: 6% is allocated for reflections and 4% is distributed to the staking pool.

The 6% allocated for reflections is distributed among token holders. Holders of SWIFT Inu tokens passively earn reflections on every transaction, regardless of whether they are buying or selling. The amount of reflections received is proportional to the size of their SWIFT holdings.

In other words, the larger the amount of SWIFT tokens held, the greater the reflections earned. This mechanism incentivizes token holders to accumulate more SWIFT tokens, as it directly impacts their potential earnings from reflections.

The remaining 4% of the tax is directed to the staking pool. This pool serves as a reward mechanism for participants who choose to lock their SWIFT Inu tokens and participate in the staking program.

By locking their tokens in the staking pool, holders not only contribute to the stability and liquidity of the ecosystem but also increase their chances of receiving a larger portion of the 4% distributed to stakers.

NFT Mining

In the SWIFT ecosystem, NFT Mining refers to a process that allows users to earn passive income through the yield of NFT Collectibles.

Although the term "mining" may be misleading, it is used metaphorically rather than referring to traditional cryptocurrency mining.

When users hold NFTs in their wallets, such as Metamask or Swifter Wallet, they are eligible to receive tokens as a reward. These tokens are generated and distributed directly to the wallet of the NFT holder, without the need for manual claiming.

The uniqueness of this mechanism lies in its decentralized nature. SWIFT aims to maximize decentralization, and therefore, tasks or actions required to claim tokens are not part of our goal.

NFT holders can simply hold their NFTs in their wallets and passively earn tokens on a daily basis.

This passive income model provides an opportunity for NFT holders to benefit from their holdings without active participation in additional tasks or processes.

By embracing this approach, SWIFT allows users to enjoy the rewards of their NFT ownership while prioritizing decentralization and simplicity in token distribution.

NFT Mining, despite its name, does not involve traditional mining activities. Instead, it is a simplified process facilitated by three smart contracts. Allow me to explain how this process works.

The first smart contract involved is the Airdrop Smart Contract. Its purpose is to distribute tokens to NFT holders. To initiate this distribution, a script is employed to automatically call the Airdrop Smart Contract.

Once the Airdrop Smart Contract is called by the script, it proceeds to invoke the mint function from the Token Smart Contract. The Token Smart Contract plays a crucial role in this process. It retrieves all the addresses of NFT holders from the NFT Smart Contract.

After gathering the addresses of NFT holders, the Token Smart Contract then proceeds to mint tokens. The minted tokens are then distributed through the Airdrop Smart Contract. This distribution mechanism ensures that the tokens reach the rightful NFT holders.

It is important to note that this entire process occurs automatically, without the need for users to claim their tokens actively. The tokens are minted and distributed to NFT holders seamlessly, simplifying the process and eliminating the requirement for manual actions.

The term "NFT Mining" in this context refers to a process that involves the automatic distribution of tokens to NFT holders.

Through the interaction of the Airdrop Smart Contract, Token Smart Contract, and NFT Smart Contract, tokens are minted and distributed without the need for users to claim them manually.
