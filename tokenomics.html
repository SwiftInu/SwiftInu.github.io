SWIFT Inu is a cryptocurrency that operates on the Binance Smart Chain. With a maximum supply of 100 million tokens.

40% of its token supply, equivalent to 40 million tokens, will be locked for a remarkable duration of 100 years in a liquidity pool. This measure ensures a stable and secure environment for the token's ecosystem.

Designed with a long-term vision in mind, SWIFT Inu aims to provide a reliable and sustainable digital asset for its community.

The locking of a significant portion of the token supply on the liquidity pool demonstrates the project's commitment to maintaining liquidity and minimizing potential market fluctuations.

By running on the Binance Smart Chain, SWIFT Inu benefits from the advanced capabilities and infrastructure of one of the leading blockchain networks.

This allows for efficient transactions and seamless integration with other decentralized applications (dApps) within the Binance Smart Chain ecosystem.
