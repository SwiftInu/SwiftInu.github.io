What is SWIFT Inu?

SWIFT Inu is an innovative Crypto project that aims to provide passive income through Reflections,Staking and NFT Miner.

What is Reflections?

In SWIFT Inu, reflections are when you get rewarded with more tokens just for holding $SWF. It's like getting a bonus for keeping your SWIFT Inu, without having to do anything else.

What is Staking?

Staking SWIFT Inu means locking up or holding your $SWF in a specific wallet to support the network's operations. By doing so, you earn rewards or interest in the form of additional tokens. It's a way to contribute to the security and functionality of the network while earning passive income.

What is NFT Miner?

NFT Miner is not actually a miner, rather the NFTs are use for distributing new minted tokens to all NFT holders. And in SWIFT Inu, NFT Miner is actually minting new tokens and distribute it automatically without the need of claiming.

How to Mine Tokens With NFT Mining?

Mining Tokens with NFT Mining of SWIFT Inu is never been this easy! To start earning tokens with NFT Mining, All you need to do is hold the NFT on your either Metamask or Trust Wallet and the tokens will be minted and sent directly on your wallet..

Do i have to claim the tokens from NFT Mining?

No! After holding the NFT on your wallet the tokens are automatocally minted everyday and sent directly on your wallet.. No need for claiming.
